Welcome To What's Up SA!

We are a user-submission powered event aggregator focused on San Antonio, striving to connect the community through providing a platform where users can easily access local events.

We understand that we aren't able to attend events presently due to shelter-in-place but that doesn't stop our passion for connecting the community! We are listing live-streamed events as well as events to look forward to once we're able to gather again. We hope to see you out there! - What's Up SA
